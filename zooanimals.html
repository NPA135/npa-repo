Zoo Animals

Bears

A bear is a large, heavy mammal that walks on the soles of its feet, having thick fur and a very short tail. Bears are related to the dog family but most species are omnivorous.

Giraffes

A giraffe is a large African mammal with a very long neck and forelegs, having a coat patterned with brown patches separated by lighter lines. It is the tallest living animal.

Lions

A lion is a large tawny-colored cat that lives in prides, found in Africa and northwestern India. The male has a flowing shaggy mane and takes little part in hunting, which is done cooperatively by the females.

A monkey is a small to medium-sized primate that typically has a long tail, most kinds of which live in trees in tropical countries.

An alligator is a large semiaquatic reptile similar to a crocodile but with a broader and shorter head, native to the Americas and China.
